Our Concept

A modern studio,
reimagined.

MIS Beauty Coworking is Vienna's intimate beauty studio where independent nail artists come together under one roof. Located in the heart of the city near Wien Mitte, we offer a refined, immaculate space designed for professionals who value their craft — and for clients who deserve more than a routine appointment.

It is not just a salon. It is a small escape — clean, modern, and warm — where every visit feels like a quiet moment of care in a busy city.
